## The ordinal numbers from 1 to 1000 are these:
* 1 пръвъ (prъvъ)
* 2 въторыи (vъtoryi)
* 3 третии (tretii)
* 4 четвьртьи (četvrtii)
* 5 пѧтыи (pętyi)
* 6 šestyi
* 7 sedmyi
* 8 osmyi
* 9 devętyi
* 100 sъtnyi
* 1000 tysęštnyi

These are adjectives and decline in all cases and numbers like other adjectives, using both simple and definite forms.
