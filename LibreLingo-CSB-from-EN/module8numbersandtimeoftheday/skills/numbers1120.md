## The numbers from 10 to 20 are these:
* 10 dzesãc
* 11 jedenôscë
* 12 dwanôscë
* 13 trzinôscë
* 14 sztërnôscë
* 15 piãtnôscë
* 16 szesnôscë
* 17 sétmënôscë
* 18 òsmnôscë
* 19 dzewiãtnôscë
* 20 dwadzescãt

These numbers always require the Genitive plural case for the following noun.

* *Example:* sétmënôscë domów (Genitive plural) - seventeen houses.
