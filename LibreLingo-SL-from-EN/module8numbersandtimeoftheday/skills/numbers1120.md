## The numbers from 10 to 20 are these:
* 10 deset
* 11 enajst
* 12 dvanajst
* 13 trinajst
* 14 štirinajst
* 15 petnajst
* 16 šestnajst
* 17 sedemnajst
* 18 osemnajst
* 19 devetnajst
* 20 dvajset

These numbers always require the Genitive plural case for the following noun.

* *Example:* sedemnajst hiš (Genitive plural) - seventeen houses.
