## The numbers from 10 to 20 are these:
* 10 źaseś
* 11 jadenasćo
* 12 dwanaśco
* 13 tśinasćo
* 14 styrnasćo
* 15 pěśnasćo
* 16 šesnasćo
* 17 sedymnasćo
* 18 wósymnasćo
* 19 źewjeśnasćo
* 20 dwaceśi

These numbers always require the Genitive plural case for the following noun.

* *Example:* sedymnasćo domow (Genitive plural) - seventeen houses.
