## The numbers from 10 to 20 are these:
* 10 deset
* 11 jedanaest
* 12 dvanaest
* 13 trinaest
* 14 četrnaest
* 15 petnaest
* 16 šesnaest
* 17 sedamnaest
* 18 osamnaest
* 19 devetnaest
* 20 dvadeset

These numbers always require the Genitive plural case for the following noun.

* *Example:* sedamnaest kuć**a** (Genitive plural) - seventeen houses.
